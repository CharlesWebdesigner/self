About Me

As a UI/UX designer, I am passionate about creating engaging and intuitive experiences that delight users. With a strong background in both user research and design, I specialize in bridging the gap between user needs and business goals. I believe that every design decision should be informed by data, and I am committed to using a user-centered approach to create solutions that meet both user and business objectives. I have experience working on a variety of projects, from small startups to large corporations, and I am always eager to tackle new challenges and push the boundaries of what is possible in UI/UX design.
During my years of professional development, I have been able to gain professional and practical knowledge of the following programming language and tools.


Languages: HTML,CSS,JAVASCRIPT,PHP,MY SQL

TOOLS: Wireframing and Prototyping

SKILLS: Visual communication skills, Analytic skills and Information Architecture skills

Design Tools Figma, Adobe Photoshop
